Advertising - FAQ
main | details | contact | faq | photos

Can I re-use a sign for future advertisements?

Yes! The type of sign we use is designed to stand up to constant outdoor exposure, and will often least a few years. As long as the sign is still in good condition (and the information printed on it is still current), you can re-use it in future campaigns.

There is no in-house graphic designer in my department. How can I get help?

Please contact either Creative Services or the Market King. They can assist you with your design process. If they have any questions, or you need more help, please contact the Marketing Coordinator.

Can I display different signs or change them as needed?

Yes. The Marketing Coordinator will help you assess your needs and determine what kind of contract best suits your needs, and will help you find the resources to meet them. Call or email the Marketing Coordinator for more information.

How long must I commit to advertise?

Most of our advertisers commit to advertising by the semester; if you have other needs, we can accommodate those as well, pending availability.

How flexible is the pricing?

Our rates for the advertising space are fixed, although there are two discounts available (on-campus groups and semester block discounts). The price of printing your ads, while fairly static, can be reduced a little; talk to your graphic designer or the Marketing Coordinator for more information.

Who is eligible to advertise?

On-campus organizations, including offices/departments, student businesses, RSOs, etc.; and off-campus groups, including businesses, community groups, and other similar organizations are all welcome to request space. If your group doesn't fit those criteria, contact the Marketing Coordinator.
