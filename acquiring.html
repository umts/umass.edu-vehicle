Acquiring or Disposing of State Vehicles

Acquisition Process:

Departments wishing to acquire a state vehicle will need to complete a Vehicle Review Board form in addition to the usual procurement process.

See Procurement Polices

Sale or Disposal of Vehicles:

Vehicles can be used as a trade-in as part of the purchase of a new vehicle or disposed of using the surplus equipment process.

See Procurement Polices
