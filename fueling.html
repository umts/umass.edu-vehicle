FUELING STATE VEHICLES
The fuel pumps are located in the North West corner of the campus within the BUS GARAGE compound at 255 Holdsworth Way. Click Here For Directions

To use the pumps for fueling a Campus Vehicle, you will need:

1.) A User ID (Your UCard campus ID number) and your PIN code.
2.) The vehicle information card for the vehicle you will be fueling.


To setup your access to the fuel pumps, get you ID and PIN code or Vehicle card:

1.) Stop by Fleet Services at Physical Plant with your UCard. Have the "M" number of the vehicle you will be driving with you or call Fleet Services at (413)545-0606.
2.) To get a NEW vehicle card, you will need to fill out a request form and fax it to Fleet Services.
3.) To request a replacement vehicle card, call Fleet Services at (413)545-0606.
