Fuel IDs

Approved fuelers: Your Fuel ID or "Driver ID" will be the Last Six Digits of your Campus ID. You will be able to locate your Campus ID on your UCard.

Staff ID Back Staff ID Front

If you have a Faculty/Staff ID Card, you will need to look at the back of the card. You can find the ID on the left hand side of the card.

Student ID

If you have a Student ID Card, you will be able to locate your Fuel ID on the front left side of your UCard.
