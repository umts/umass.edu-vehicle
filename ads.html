Advertising - Main
main | details | contact | faq | photos

The UMass Amherst Advertising on Vehicles Program is a venue for both on- and off- campus advertisers wishing to target the UMass Amherst student population. We sell sign space to qualified advertisers on our Campus Services trucks, which circulate throughout campus during the day. The advertising space available on these trucks is seen by a community of over 25,000 students, staff, and faculty every weekday of the semester, and so is an excellent way to target the UMass Amherst, and only the UMass Amherst, community.

We strive to meet your needs. We offer a very specific audience in the university community, we can assist you in printing and design, our contracts are flexible, and the advertising costs are inexpensive and very cost effective. In short, we offer affordable advertising designed to suit your needs.

Not sure where to start? Contact us, and we'll help you with every aspect of the process - from drafting signs to printing them - and when you're all ready, we'll install them for you. Note that this is a separate service than advertising on buses. To advertise on buses contact PVTA.
