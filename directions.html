DIRECTIONS

We're located in the northwest corner of UMass campus between Computer Science and Parking Services:


View Larger Map

Directions from Amherst Center:

Head toward UMass on North Pleasant Street. At the intersection of North Pleasant Street and Massachusetts Avenue, go straight onto Massachusetts Ave. At the first set of lights, take a right onto Commonwealth Avenue. Go through one set of traffic lights, and take a right at the next set of lights onto Holdsworth Way. Transit is located immediately on the left.

Directions from North Amherst Center:

Head toward UMass on North Pleasant Street. At the intersection of North Pleasant Street and Governor's Drive, take a right onto Governor's Drive. At the next set of lights, take a left onto Holdsworth Way. Transit is located immediately on the left.
